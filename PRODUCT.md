# Trump Predictor

## What it is

A single-page AI chat application for exploring how Donald Trump has historically responded to geopolitical and economic events — and for reasoned scenario analysis on new situations. Covers Term 1 (2017–2021) and Term 2 (2025–present).

## Who it's for

Policy analysts, journalists, researchers, market watchers, and curious citizens who want analytical — not partisan — conversation.

## Register

Product UI (tool). Design serves the product.

## Primary action

Send a question in the chat composer.

## Tone

Neutral, analytical, newsroom-terminal seriousness. Not campaign material.

## Backend status

UI and streaming pipeline are live in **demo mode**. Connect your backend in `lib/ai/chat-handler.ts`.
